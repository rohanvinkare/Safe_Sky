AQI Range Air Pollution Level Health Implications Cautionary Statement (for PM 2.5)
0 - 50 Good Air quality is considered satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no risk None
51 - 100 Moderate Air quality is acceptable; however, for some pollutants there may be a moderate health concern for a very small number of people who are unusually sensitive to air pollution. Active children and adults, and people with respiratory disease, such as asthma, should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.
101-150 Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups Members of sensitive groups may experience health effects. The general public is not likely to be affected. Active children and adults, and people with respiratory disease, such as asthma, should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.
151-200 Unhealthy Everyone may begin to experience health effects; members of sensitive groups may experience more serious health effects. Active children and adults, and people with respiratory disease, such as asthma, should avoid prolonged outdoor exertion; everyone else, especially children, should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.
201-300 Very Unhealthy Health warnings of emergency conditions. The entire population is more likely to be affected. Active children and adults, and people with respiratory disease, such as asthma, should avoid all outdoor exertion; everyone else, especially children, should limit outdoor exertion.
300+ Hazardous Health alert: everyone may experience more serious health effects. Everyone should avoid all outdoor exertion.

About Us

Our team is dedicated to developing a cutting-edge software solution that is pivotal in ensuring industry compliance with government-mandated safety standards. We have created a robust system that establishes the benchmark for safety protocols, particularly in the realm of aerial hazard monitoring. Our goal is to empower industries to effectively meet regulatory requirements and elevate safety measures to new heights.

  What we do?

We specialize in developing and implementing a software solution that is instrumental in enabling industries to comply with government-mandated safety standards. Our focus is on providing a robust system that sets the gold standard for safety protocols, particularly in the area of aerial hazard monitoring. By doing so, we aim to enhance safety measures and ensure that industries can effectively meet regulatory requirements.

 Why choose us?

Our team stands out for our expertise in developing a software solution that is at the forefront of ensuring industry compliance with safety standards. We are dedicated to providing a robust system that enables industries to meet regulatory requirements effectively, particularly in the critical area of aerial hazard monitoring. By choosing us, industries can benefit from our commitment to enhancing safety measures and ensuring compliance with government-mandated safety standards.

 Where are we?

Our team is based at IIIT Sri City. However, our software solution is designed to be accessible and applicable to industries across various locations, enabling them to enhance safety measures and comply with government-mandated safety standards regardless of their geographical location.
